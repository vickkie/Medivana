ABOUT US

Promo Kings was founded in 2015 and is one stop shop for branding and promotion items, t-shirts, Caps umbrella, aprons, bibs overall, bags gadget, drinking ware and much more. Promo Kings is setup in collaboration with Kings Wear limited popularly known as Kings Collections.

Come 2019 Promo Kings Limited decided to expand its product range to large format and outdoor advertising, which includes Signboards, billboards, Banners, Teardrop banners, Roll up banners, Gondolas, Direct printing on ABS Corex , Forex Perspex and Alucaboard, Promotional Tables and much more not to mention all.

We have invested heavily in our state of the art machinery and technology. With world class operating equipment sourced from around the world including Europe, USA, and Japan we are equipped to provide our clients with a wide range of innovative and complex branding possibilities.

Sourcing of quality and innovative merchandise is a key strength for promo kings which has allowed us to grow rapidly. Our capacities ensure we hold sufficient stock to cater to even the most urgent brand requirements.
